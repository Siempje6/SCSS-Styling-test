Working at Reflection

Reflection’s mission is to build frontier open intelligence and make it accessible to all.

We’re developing open weight models for individuals, agents, enterprises, and even nation states. Our team of AI researchers and company builders come from DeepMind, OpenAI, Google Brain, Meta, Character.AI, Anthropic and beyond.

We’re hiring in San Francisco, New York, and London, with relocation support available for those moving from other areas.

Many of the problems we tackle require real-time alignment and fast resolution. We’ve found this is best achieved in-person.

What we offer

We believe that to build superintelligence that is truly open, you need to start at the foundation. Joining Reflection means building from the ground up as part of a small talent-dense team. You will help define our future as a company, and help define the frontier of open foundational models.

We want you to do the most impactful work of your career with the confidence that you and the people you care about most are supported.

Values and principles

Defining and building the frontier of open weight models is a vast opportunity. It’s a responsibility we take seriously, and it’s something we feel fortunate to work on. There is a lot to do. We focus on the most meaningful problems and work with urgency while holding ourselves to a few principles.

  • Top-tier compensation: Salary and equity structured to recognize and retain the best talent globally.

  • Health & wellness: Comprehensive medical, dental, vision, life and disability insurance.

  • Life & family: Life & family: Fully paid parental leave for all new parents, including adoptive and surrogate journeys. Financial support for family planning.

  • Benefits & balance: Paid time off when you need it, relocation support, and perks that optimize your time.

  • Opportunities to connect with teammates: Lunch and dinner are provided in the office daily. We have regular off-sites and team celebrations.
